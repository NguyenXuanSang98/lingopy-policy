Privacy Policy

Lingopy ("we", "our", "us") operates the Lingopy mobile application ("the App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email, name)
  • Learning data (progress, vocabulary history)
  • Device information (device ID, OS version)
  • Analytics data via Google Firebase

Use of Information

We use the collected data to improve user experience, personalize lessons, analyze app usage, and send notifications related to learning progress.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share anonymous analytics data with third-party services such as Google Firebase.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request data deletion at any time.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted here.
